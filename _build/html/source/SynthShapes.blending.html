Blend tensors (shapes into image) using blending parameter.

Parameters:

  • alpha (Sampler or float) -- Blending factor or sampler.

  • intensity_shift (Sampler or float) -- Intensity offset WRT mean=0, std=0 background.

forward(foreground, background, mask)[source]

Perform blending operation.

Parameters:

  • foreground (tensor) -- The tensor with shapes to blend (each shape should have a unique ID).

  • background (tensor) -- The intensity image (tensor) to blend shapes into.

  • alpha (float) -- Weight of the shape tensor. Larger magnitude = more blob character. Tip: alpha > 1 maintains blob texture well.
